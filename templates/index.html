  • Install MetaMask.
  • Once installed, switch MetaMask to the Rinkeby Test Network and create an account.
  • With your Rinkeby testnet Ethereum account, get some testnet tokens at the faucet here.
  • Grab an audio sample and head over to /create to create your first SoundChain NFT!
